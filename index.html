Team

Meet the Managing Director

Debo Adedeji (arpa)

Managing Director

Debo Adedeji is an astute media and advertising practitioner with 12 years experience spanning Abuja, Lagos, Oyo and Kwara states. He started his broadcasting journey at Hot FM, Apo (Abuja FCT) where he became notable as the top male voice on radio in the capital city. His eloquence, fluency and intellectual prowess quickly distinguished him across all media houses in the capital city. Debo worked with Hot fm, Kiss Fm, Rhythm FM and eventually continued with Raypower FM, Kwara State.

Following his impressive display of leadership abilities, he was appointed the General Manager/COO of Gravity 88.1 FM, Igboho, Oyo state. Here, he utilized his vast experience in radio broadcasting to establish Gravity FM amongst the Top 5 radio stations in Oyo state. He was awarded ‘General Manager of the Year’ in Oyo state during his 2-year stint with Gravity FM. Debo went on to become the Chief Executive Officer of Beauld Communications Limited, largely involved in outdoor advertising, with over 40 billboards across Kwara state. This company was actively engaged in the APC publicity in Ilorin & Kwara South which recorded significant outcome for the party.

He is currently the Chairman of the Advertising Practitioners’ Association, Kwara State Chapter which has begun to record giant strides in enhancing the professional practice of Advertising in Kwara state. Debo is an alumnus of the prestigious King’s College Lagos. He later went on to bag a Bachelor’s Degree in Physiotherapy from the renowned Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. He also possesses two Post Graduate degrees – Masters in Public Health (MPH) from the University of Ilorin, and Masters in Business Administration (MBA) from the Kwara State Univeristy. He is a licensed Advertising Practitioner as he is an associate member of the Advertising Practitioners’ Council of Nigeria (APCON).
Debo is happily married to Dr. Titilayo Adedeji and they are blessed with children.
